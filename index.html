Our Profile

About Our School


SMAN 1 CIRUAS is a public high school located in the Ciruas District, Serang Regency, Banten. SMAN 1 CIRUAS was established on November 20, 1984, with the establishment decree number 0558/0/1984, under the Ministry of Education and Culture. In its educational activities, this school, which has 1,481 students, is guided by 65 professional teachers in their respective fields. The current principal of SMAN 1 CIRUAS is Yanto, and the responsible operator is Sukarno. This school has also been accredited with an A rating, with accreditation decree number 755/BAN-SM/SK/2019, dated September 9, 2019.

Vision & Mission

Vision:

To be a center of educational excellence that empowers students to become innovative thinkers, compassionate leaders, and responsible global citizens.

Mission:

To foster a community of lifelong learners through innovative teaching methods, comprehensive curriculum, and collaborative learning experiences.

Achievement

Academic Excellence

Our students have won the Business Plan competition at the Woderkind Festival and have also achieved gold and bronze medals in the 2023 National Student Research Olympiad (OPSI)..

Sports Championships

In the field of sports, our students are actively excelling in athletics, participating in events such as O2SN and the Tangerang Open. Additionally, we have sent representatives to various championships, including SAC Indonesia..

Outstanding in the field of arts.

SMAN 1 Ciruas has achieved recognition in the field of cultural arts, one of which is the establishment of Rampak Bedug as the school's local excellence. In addition, the students of SMAN 1 Ciruas have also garnered the judges' attention with their research in the fields of cultural arts and history, focusing on the theme of the city of Surosowan Banten.
